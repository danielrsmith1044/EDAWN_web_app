About the Program

What Is Business Builders?

The EDAWN Business Builders program is a volunteer-based outreach initiative focused on Business Retention and Expansion (BRE) across Greater Reno-Sparks. Relaunched in Spring 2025, the program mobilizes EDAWN investor-volunteers to directly engage with existing businesses in the region.

Business Builder volunteers work alongside EDAWN's program staff to visit targeted companies, listen to their needs, connect them with resources, and help remove barriers to growth. The program currently focuses on key industry groups including finance and logistics, with findings and recommendations presented to stakeholders each quarter.

EDAWN's mission is to add quality jobs to the region, support the success of existing companies, assist newly forming businesses, and diversify the economy — all while positively impacting the quality of life in Northern Nevada.

The Process

How the Program Works

Business Builders volunteers play a direct role in strengthening Northern Nevada's economy through structured company outreach and support.

1
Get Assigned

Volunteers are paired with targeted local businesses based on industry focus areas identified by EDAWN.

2
Make Contact

Reach out to your assigned companies via phone, email, or in-person to introduce the program and schedule a visit.

3
Visit & Listen

Conduct one-on-one visits to understand business challenges, growth goals, and what support they need most.

4
Connect Resources

Link businesses with workforce training, community partners, economic development tools, and EDAWN's broader network.

Who Can Participate

Is This Program For You?

The Business Builders program is designed for EDAWN investors and community partners who are passionate about Northern Nevada's economic future and want to make a tangible difference.

EDAWN Investors & Partners

Members at any investment level are welcome to participate as volunteer Business Builders.

Community Leaders & Professionals

Business professionals, executives, and civic leaders who want to give back to the regional economy.

Industry Advocates

Those with expertise in finance, logistics, manufacturing, or other key Northern Nevada industries.
